### Oauth2

- **Type**: OAuth
- **Flow**: implicit
- **Authorization URL**: https://accounts.google.com/o/oauth2/auth
- **Scopes**: 
 - **https://www.googleapis.com/auth/display-video**: Create, see, edit, and permanently delete your Display & Video 360 entities and reports
